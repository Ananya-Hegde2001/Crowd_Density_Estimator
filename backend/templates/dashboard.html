How It Works

1

Camera Setup

Connect your camera and configure the monitoring area. Our system supports various camera types and automatically optimizes video quality for best detection results.

2

AI Processing

Advanced YOLOv8 neural network analyzes each frame in real-time, detecting and counting people with 95%+ accuracy. The AI continuously learns and improves.

3

Zone Analysis

The monitoring area is divided into a 3x3 grid system. Each zone is independently analyzed for crowd density levels: Low, Medium, High, and Critical.

4

Alert Generation

When density thresholds are exceeded, the system generates instant alerts with visual and audio notifications. All alerts are logged for historical analysis.
